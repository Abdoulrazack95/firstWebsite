Destinations

Djibouti is a country with amazing charms. Its salt lakes, its coasts bordered by warm sea and its desert landscapes with incomparable beauties make tourism an exhilarating activity. Breathtaking landscapes where the desert expanses give way to limestone chimneys. The relief of the country offers hiking enthusiasts, a spectacular view of its salt lakes, favorite places for flamingos and pelicans. So Here are the most unique places that Djibouti offers:

Lac Assal

Lake Assal,located -155 meters below sea level, enclosed in a wild basin dominated by austere mountains, is the lowest point on the African continent. The Lake Assal region is undoubtedly one of the hottest on the planet with temperatures up to 50°C.

Ardoukoba Volcano

Ardoukoba is the latest addition to the world's volcanoes. The eruption lasted only a week. This volcanic area results from the slow opening of an ocean between Arabia and Africa. This partially emerged rift course from the Dead Sea to Mozambique. Djibouti sits at a triple junction between the Red Sea, East African and Indian Ocean rifts.

Lake Abbé

You will never forget your first view of Lake Abbé!.
Lake Abbé is a huge field of limestone chimneys, some standing as high as 100m, belching out puffs of steam. Lake Abbé and its magical landscapes. When the sun sets behind the limestone chimneys, the landscape takes on the appearance of a fantastic world. Lake Abbé located 140km southwest of Djibouti City it is often described as a slice of moon on the crust of earth.

Swim with the Gentile Giants of the Sea

The whale shark (Rhincodon Typus), which can exceptionally reach 20 meters in length, with a mass of 34 tonnes, is considered to be the largest large fish currently living on the planet. Its size is nevertheless between 4 and 14 meters. The whale shark that loves tropical waters is at home in Djibouti. The Whale Shark is easy to meet, observe and photograph.
Certainly, swimming alongside him, when his mouth is open, is simply magical. When you find yourself face to face with this majestic mammal, time “suspends its flight” and the moment becomes unforgettable. Experience trying at least once in your life.
